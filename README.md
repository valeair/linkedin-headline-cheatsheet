## 1. 📐 Headline Formulas (Quick Reference)

| Formula                             | Example                                             |
| ----------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| `Role \| Value / Outcome`           | Sales Manager \| Grew revenue 40% in 2 years        |
| `Skills \| Industry \| Achievement` | Python, SQL \| Fintech \| Built 3 data pipelines    |
| `Your Title + Who You Help`         | Resume Writer for Creative Professionals            |
| `Experience + Future Goal`          | Marketing Analyst → Product Marketing Manager       |
| `Keyword Stack (for search)`        | Supply Chain \| Logistics \| Inventory Optimization |
| `Personal Brand Statement`          | Turn complex data into simple stories               |

## 2. 📝 Templates by Experience Level & Role

### 🎓 Students / Fresh Graduates
- `[Degree] in [Field] \| [University] \| Passionate about [Interest]`  
  *Example:* BA in Marketing, NYU \| Social media & content creator
- `Aspiring [Role] \| [Top Skill 1] + [Top Skill 2] \| Looking for [Type of role]`  
  *Example:* Aspiring Product Manager \| UX research + SQL \| Seeking 2025 internships

### 💼 Experienced Professionals (5+ years)
- `[Senior Role] \| [Core skill 1], [Skill 2] \| [Metric-driven result]`  
  *Example:* Senior DevOps Engineer \| AWS, Kubernetes \| Reduced deployment time by 50%
- `[Industry] Specialist \| Helping [audience] [achieve goal]`  
  *Example:* B2B SaaS Specialist \| Helping startups hit $1M ARR through outbound sales

### 🔄 Career Changers
- `Past [old role] → Future [new role] \| Transferable skills: [skill 1], [skill 2]`  
  *Example:* Former Teacher → Junior Developer \| Problem-solving + Communication + Python
- `[New role] (ex‑[old industry]) \| Bringing [unique perspective]`  
  *Example:* UX Designer (ex‑Hospitality) \| Designing empathy‑driven experiences

## 3. 🔄 Before & After: Short Case Studies

### Case 1: Generic → Specific
- **Before:** `Software Engineer at XYZ Corp`  
- **After:** `Full‑Stack Engineer | React, Node.js | Built 5 production apps`  
- **Why it works:** Adds skills and quantifiable result.

### Case 2: Student with no experience
- **Before:** `Student at State University`  
- **After:** `Finance Student | Excel, Financial Modeling | CFA Level 1 Candidate`  
- **Why it works:** Highlights relevant skills and certification in progress.

### Case 3: Career changer
- **Before:** `Accountant looking for new opportunities`  
- **After:** `Data Analyst (ex‑Accountant) | SQL, Tableau | Turning numbers into insights`  
- **Why it works:** Bridges past and future, shows transferable strength.

## 4. ⚠️ Common Mistakes (and Fixes)

| Mistake                               | Why it hurts                         | Fix                                                          |
| ------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Only your job title                   | Recruiters can’t find your keywords  | Add 2–3 core skills or a value statement                     |
| “Open to work” (no other context)     | Missed chance to show what you offer | Combine with specialty: *Open to Product Marketing roles \| GTM strategy + Analytics* |
| Too vague: “Hardworking professional” | Means nothing to recruiters          | Replace with concrete fact: *Reduced logistics costs by 15%* |
